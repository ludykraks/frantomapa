About Frantomapa Educational Aid

Frantomapa Educational Aid is a Non Profit Charity Organisation in Ghana – West Africa that aims to nurture critical thinkers and innovators to propel the much needed human and technological development of Ghana.

Frantomapa is organised by a group of concerned young educationists from Ghana and the United Kingdom.

We operate under the Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 4 and 5 respectively.

Thus, quality education and by its regulations, it is required to carry on the business of:

  1. Donating educational materials and facilities to less endowed schools and communities.
  2. Assisting brilliant but needy children to get quality education.
  3. Organising of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) Quiz competition and exhibition.
  4. Counseling and mentorship program.
  5. Publishing of educational materials.

The organisation believes disciplines like Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) are inevitable in the educational journey and day to day activities of life.

It is therefore our major goal to propel this development through our works in Ghana.

Why Frantomapa Educational Aid?

Frantomapa came into existence as a result of pupils poor performance in Mathematics, Science & Technology over the years which resulted in Ghana being ranked last in Mathematics, Science & Technology among 76 countries on the 12th of May, 2015.

We also realised that our pupils hardly innovate to solve problems.
