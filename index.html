Frantomapa Educational Aid

Nurturing critical thinkers and innovators.

Our primary goal is to ensure underprivileged Ghanaian children keep learning no matter what.

Who We Are

Frantomapa Educational Aid is an NGO (non-governmental organisation) formed in Ghana on the 8th May, 2016.

Our registered office and principal place of business is in Accra, Ghana.

How We Are Helping

Grand Opening of Technology Centre (2021)

In respect to our vision and mission which is based of STEM especially Technology, the Frantomapa birthed the Rural Technology Project by establishing a Technology Center in the rural areas of Ghana.

Workshop on Classroom Management (2019)

Workshop on Classroom Management, Teaching and Learning Resources preparation and jolly phonics for teachers in Belcoff School Complex- Akropong Akuapem.

Reconstruction of Classroom Block (2017)

In early 2017, we came across a dilapidated school building which children of Tinkong Presbyterian KG were using as a classroom. It was an eye sore and a deathtrap for the innocent kids. We took it upon ourselves to raise funds to build a new classroom block for the kids.

Your Gift Fuels Our Mission

Together, we can provide these kids with access to good education, proper infrastruction, learning facilities and guidance they need to become critical thinkers and innnovators.
