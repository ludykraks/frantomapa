Kidpreneurship Challenge (2023)

Theme: "Your Passion Must Pay You"

Frantomapa Educational Aid (Ghana) in collaboration with Ultra Education (UK) is happy to announce the maiden edition of the Kidpreneurship Challenge (2023).

This year's theme is "Your Passion Must Pay You."

The aim of Kidpreneurship Challenge is to promote entrepreneurship among young people.

How to Join the Challenge

Click on this Kidpreneurship Challenge link to begin your challenge.

Kidpreneurs taking part in this challenge must be between the ages of 10 - 16 years.

For more information contact us at 055 391 7555 (Ghana).

Why Frantomapa Educational Aid?

Frantomapa came into existence as a result of pupils poor performance in Mathematics, Science & Technology over the years which resulted in Ghana being ranked last in Mathematics, Science & Technology among 76 countries on the 12th of May, 2015.

We also realised that our pupils hardly innovate to solve problems.
